🔤 Trim Cap Letters

Ideal for storefronts, malls, corporate buildings, and illuminated signboards, trim cap letters create a polished and luxurious look. They are durable, weather-resistant, and customizable to match any branding style or color theme.

  • 3D effect with illuminated acrylic faces
  • Durable aluminum or PVC side trims
  • LED lighting for bright, energy-efficient signage
  • Customizable fonts, colors, and sizes
  • Weather-resistant for long-term outdoor use

🎯 Use Cases

  • Retail stores and shopping malls
  • Hotels and corporate office buildings
  • Showrooms and exhibition centers
  • Healthcare and educational institutions
