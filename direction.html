🧭 Directional Sign Boards

Directional signs help visitors navigate large areas such as malls, offices, hospitals, or campuses. Acrylic boards offer clarity and durability while enhancing the space’s aesthetics.

✅ Key Features

  • Bold arrows and universal icons
  • Clear, legible fonts
  • Mounted or suspended based on location
  • Weather-resistant for indoor/outdoor use

📌 Common Uses

  • Hospital wayfinding signs
  • Floor maps in malls
  • Office floor directions
  • Exhibition and event signage
